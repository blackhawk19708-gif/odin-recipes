About The Dish

Lasagna is a baked Italian dish consisting of wide strips of pasta cooked and layered with meat, cheese, and tomato sauce.

And in this tutorial, you'll learn how to create a spectacular dish of lasagna for you, your family, and friends

Ingridients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Two cans of Crushed tomatoes
  • Tomato
  • Two cans of Tomato Purée
  • Tomato paste
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Ricotta Eggs
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Parsley Garlic
  • Basil
  • Chopped Meat
  • Water
  • Mozzarella Cheese

Steps

  1. Get a sauce pan and pour olive oil till it covers bottom of your pan.
  2. Chop a clove of garlic and throw it in with the olive oil till it turns brown.
  3. Now take your ground beef and put in in the pan till it is brown.
  4. Then pour in two cans of crushed tomatoes, two cans of tomato purée, and one can oftomato paste.
  5. Put one cup of water and dump it in the pan with the sauce.
  6. Add tablespoon of salt, and a tablespoon of pepper.
  7. And cook the sauce for an hour on low.
  8. Grab a pot and fill with water.
  9. Grab the lasgna noodles and throw them in the boiling water.
  10. In a separate bowl add a large container of 2 ricotta eggs, parsley garlic, and a cup of parmesan cheese.
  11. Get another pot and boil your noodles until al dente(slightly chewy).
  12. Then get a baking dish and lay 1 layer of noodles on the bottom and put a spoonful of ricotta and spread it out until it covers all of the first layer of lasagna. Then get a spoonful of the sauce and pour it on top of the ricotta. After, put another layer of noodles on top and repeat the process till you get the final layer.
  13. Once you reach the final layer, put a layer of noodles on top a layer of sauce then add mozzarella cheese.
