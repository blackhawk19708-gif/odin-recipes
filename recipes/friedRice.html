About The Dish

Fried rice is a popular Asian dish made from pre-cooked rice stir-fried with various ingredients.

And in this tutorial you'll learn how to create a spectacular dish of fried rice for you, your family, and friends.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 1 lb chicken breast or thigh, diced
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2-3 tbsp soy sauce
  • ½ tsp salt
  • oyster sauce
  • ½ tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • Green onions for garnish

Steps

  1. Heat 1 tbsp oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat. Add chicken and season lightly with salt and pepper. Then cook until fully done and lightly golden. Remove and set aside.
  2. Then get 2 eggs and scramble them in the pan quickly, once finished push them aside in the pot
  3. Add your garlic and mixed vegetables and str fry for 1-2 minutes till fragrant
  4. Add oyster sauce, soy sauce, and sesame oil and mix till it is evenly distributed to the rice.
  5. Add your chicken in and cook for another 2 minutes
