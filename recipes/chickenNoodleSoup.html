About The Dish

Chicken Noodle Soup has roots in ancient culinary traditions. It is believed to have originated in China, where chicken and noodles were combined in broth.

And in this tutorial you'll learn how to create a spectacular bowl of chicken noodle soup for you, your family, and friends

Ingridients

  • 1 lb chicken
  • 8 cups chicken broth
  • 2 tbsp olive oil or butter
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 2 celery stalks, sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ½ tsp black pepper
  • ½ tsp dried thyme or Italian seasoning
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 2 cups egg noodles
  • Fresh parsley

Steps

  1. In a large pot, heat your oil over medium heat.
  2. Then add chicken, season lightly with salt and pepper.
  3. Cook until lightly browned
  4. Remove and set aside.
  5. In the same pot, add onions, carrots, and celery.
  6. Cook for 4-5 minutes until softened.
  7. Add garlic> and cook 30 seconds.
  8. Pour in broth.
  9. Add thyme, bay leaf, and remaining seasoning.
  10. Bring to a gentle boil.
  11. Add chicken back in.
  12. Reduce heat and simmer for 15-20 minutes until chicken is fully cooked.
  13. Remove chicken, shred with forks, and return to pot.
  14. Add egg noodles then cook 6-8 minutes
